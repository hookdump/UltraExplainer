Why it wins

A token bucket holds a small reserve, so a client that's been quiet can spend a short burst — exactly what a paginating client or a ret‑storm does — then settles to the refill rate. Steady traffic under the rate never sees a 429.

When to pick another

Need byte-exact accounting for billing? Sliding-window log, and pay the memory. Shaping a queue to a constant drain rate? Leaky bucket. Fixed window only when simplicity outranks correctness — its boundary lets through 2× at the seam.
